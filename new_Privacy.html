Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01

in5KM respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the in5KM app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address (only if you choose to create an account)
  • Location information (with your permission)
  • Content you choose to provide, such as posts, titles, descriptions, and timestamps
  • Basic device and usage information (such as device type, operating system version, and app usage events)

2. Location Information

We collect precise location data only while the App is in use and only after you grant permission. Location data is used solely to show nearby posts based on your selected radius. The App does not collect location data in the background.

3. How We Use Information

  • To show location-based content and nearby posts
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The App displays advertisements provided by third-party services such as Google AdMob. These services may use advertising identifiers, cookies, or similar technologies to show ads that are relevant to you. Information collected by these services is handled according to their own privacy policies.

The App may also use third-party authentication services such as Google Sign-In. These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information may be shared only:

  • To operate and maintain the App (for example, through advertising or authentication services)
  • When required by law

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Request access to or deletion of your data
  • Control location permissions through your device settings
  • Use the App without creating an account

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

10. Contact
