in5km — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 August 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how in5km (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and safeguards personal information when you use our mobile app and associated services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Voluntarily provided: Information you actively provide (e.g. when creating content, contacting us).
  • Automatically collected: Device and usage data such as IP address, device type/version, app activity, and error logs.
  • Device data: Information accessed through permissions you grant (e.g. location data if enabled).
  • User-generated content: Materials (text, images, etc.) you submit for publication within the app.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the app’s features.
  • To communicate with you regarding support, updates, or feedback.
  • To monitor app usage, fix errors, and improve performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations where required.

3. Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. We encourage you to keep your account details safe.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide the app’s services or to comply with legal obligations. When no longer required, data will be deleted or anonymized.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the data promptly.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information only if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or with your consent.

7. Your Rights

  • You may request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • You may ask us to correct or delete inaccurate or outdated information.
  • You may withdraw consent for optional features at any time.
  • You may opt-out of communications by contacting us.

8. International Users

If you are outside Australia, note that your information may be processed in Australia where privacy laws may differ. We will take steps to protect your information consistent with this Policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted within the app, with the “Last updated” date changed accordingly.
