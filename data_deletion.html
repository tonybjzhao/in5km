Data Deletion Request

If you would like to request deletion of your data associated with the in5KM app, please contact us using the email below.

What data can be deleted

  • Account information (email address)
  • User-generated content (posts and related metadata)

How to request deletion

Please send an email to:

in5km.zyt@googlemail.com

Include the following information in your request:

  • The email address used to sign in
  • Your request to delete your account and associated data

Processing time

We will process data deletion requests within 30 days.
