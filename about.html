About in5KM

in5KM is a lightweight, community-driven Android app that helps people share local events, information, and requests within a short distance radius.

What is in5KM?

The idea behind in5KM is simple: most things that matter to us happen close to where we live. in5KM allows users to post and view nearby events, alerts, or help requests within a configurable local radius.

Posts can be created anonymously, encouraging open and low-pressure sharing while keeping the focus on local relevance rather than personal profiles.

Key Features

  • Location-based posts within a local radius
  • Anonymous or optional account usage
  • Simple event, alert, and help-request posting
  • Community feedback through likes and dislikes

Privacy & Transparency

in5KM respects user privacy. Location information is used only to display nearby posts and is not collected in the background.

For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

App Status

in5KM is an independent project and is actively evolving. Features, design, and policies may change as we learn from user feedback and improve the app.
