Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: 2025-01

in5KM is a community platform. To keep it safe and useful, users must follow these rules.

1. Allowed Use

You may use the App to share helpful, truthful, and lawful local information.

2. Prohibited Content

You must not post:

  • Hate speech or discriminatory content
  • Harassment, threats, or abuse
  • False or misleading emergency information
  • Spam, scams, or malicious links
  • Illegal or dangerous content

3. Reporting Violations

Users may report inappropriate content through in-app reporting tools or by contacting support.

4. Enforcement

We may remove content or suspend or permanently terminate accounts that violate this policy.

5. Responsibility

You are responsible for your content and interactions with other users.

6. Contact

For questions or reports, contact: in5km.zyt@gmail.com
