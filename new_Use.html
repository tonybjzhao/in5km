in5km — Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: 26 August 2025

This Acceptable Use Policy (“Policy”) applies to the products, services, and technologies (“Products”) provided by in5km. It is designed to protect our users, our community, and the wider Internet from unethical, irresponsible, or illegal use.

1. General Principles

Use of our Products must be fair, responsible, and consistent with applicable laws. Breaches of this Policy may result in suspension or termination of access, and in serious cases we may be required to notify authorities.

2. Customer Accountability

Users are responsible for their own actions and those of anyone using the Products under their account. Reasonable security measures must be maintained to prevent unauthorized use. Complaints regarding misuse may be directed to the account holder for resolution.

3. Prohibited Activities

3.1 Copyright & Unauthorized Content

You must not upload, share, or distribute any material that:

  • Infringes copyright, trademark, trade secrets, or other rights;
  • Is obscene, defamatory, threatening, or otherwise unlawful.

3.2 Spam & Messaging Abuse

You must not use the Products to:

  • Send unsolicited bulk or commercial messages (“spam”);
  • Run unconfirmed mailing or messaging lists;
  • Use purchased or third-party contact databases for spam purposes.

3.3 Malicious or Exploitative Use

Prohibited uses include:

  • Hacking, cracking, or unauthorized account access;
  • Distributing malware, viruses, or harmful code;
  • Denial-of-service attacks, network abuse, or harassment;
  • Publishing content that incites violence, depicts abuse, or promotes discrimination;
  • Illegal activities such as gambling, terrorism, drug or arms trafficking, or weapons development.

3.4 Misuse of in5km Brand

You must not impersonate in5km, falsely claim affiliation, or misuse our name or brand to gain trust, services, or advantage.

4. Enforcement

We reserve the right to take appropriate action — including suspending or terminating accounts — when this Policy is violated. To the extent permitted by law, no credit or refund will be provided for interruptions caused by violations.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Updates will be effective either when you next use the Products after publication within the app, or 30 days after publication, whichever comes first.
